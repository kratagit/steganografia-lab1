Technology development is accelerating every year. Many companies are investing huge sums of money in modern solutions. Artificial intelligence helps us with our daily duties. People are increasingly using smart home assistants. Process automation allows for saving a lot of valuable time. On the other hand, online security is becoming a massive challenge. Cybercriminals are coming up with ever newer attack methods. That is why education in digital hygiene is so important. Account passwords should be complex and changed regularly. Experts recommend using two-step verification wherever possible. The future of the internet will certainly bring us many more surprises. We must be ready for continuous learning and adaptation to new conditions.
